Honey Garlic Little Smokies

3 hours 5 minutes

Easy

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together brown sugar, honey, ketchup, soy sauce and garlic.

  2. Stir so they are coated evenly. Cook on HIGH for 2-3 hours or on LOW for 4 hours, stirring occasionally.

  3. These Crockpot Honey Garlic Little Smokies Sausages will be the hit of your next gathering!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz. lil' smokies or any little cocktail weenies

Nutritional Info per Serving
