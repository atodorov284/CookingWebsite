Creamy Prosciutto Cracker

11 minutes

Easy

  1. In a small bowl, mix cream cheese, garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper together. Set aside.

  2. Cut prosciutto into small rectangles.

  3. Spread a small about of mixture onto each cracker, until each cracker is thinly covered.

  4. Roll each prosciutto up and place on top of the crackers.

  5. Drizzle a small amount of honey over each and sprinkle with parsly.

Ingredients

  • 7 oz whipped cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 1/2 tsp olive oil
  • 3 ounces sliced Prosciutto
  • 40 crackers
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • finely chopped parsly for topping
  • Honey for drizzling
